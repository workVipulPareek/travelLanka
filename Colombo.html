Colombo

Colombo Lotus Tower (Nelum Kuluna)

Location: AC6, Colombo 01000, Sri Lanka

Height: 350 meters (1,150 feet)

The Lotus Tower making it the tallest self-supported structure in South Asia. This iconic tower is a significant tourist attraction, offering an observation deck with panoramic views of the city and a revolving restaurant that provides a unique dining experience with 360-degree vistas. In addition to these, the tower includes planned amusement facilities and conference and banquet halls for various events. Open throughout the week, the tower has different ticket prices for local and international visitors, with discounts for children and groups. Easily accessible from major parts of Colombo, the best times to visit are early mornings or late afternoons to avoid crowds and enjoy the best views.

Advance booking is recommended for the revolving restaurant and special events. The tower is also conveniently located near other attractions such as Galle Face Green and Beira Lake, making it a must-see landmark that blends modern architecture with cultural significance.
