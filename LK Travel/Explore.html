Districts

Colombo

The bustling commercial capital of Sri Lanka, known for its nightlife and colonial architecture...

Nestled in the hills, Kandy is renowned for the sacred Temple of the Tooth and its cultural heritage.

Badulla

Offers lush tea plantations, picturesque waterfalls, and scenic hiking trails...

Jaffna

Rich in Tamil culture, Jaffna boasts historic temples and vibrant markets...

Kegalle

Home to the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and picturesque rubber plantations...

Matale

Known for its spice gardens and the stunning Aluvihara Rock Cave Temple...

A historic city with a UNESCO World Heritage-listed Dutch Fort and beautiful beaches.

Mannar

Famous for its baobab trees, ancient history, and diverse birdlife...
